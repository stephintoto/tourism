Ernakulam

The district includes Kochi, also known as the commercial capital of Kerala, which is famous for its ancient Churches, Hindu temples, synagogues and mosques. The district includes the largest metropolitan region of the state: Greater Cochin.

Alappuzha

Also well-known as the 'Venice of the East' because of network of canals and waterways, Alappuzha is a district of immense natural beauty. It is embraced by the Arabian Sea in the west and has a network of lakes, lagoons and freshwater rivers criss-crossing it.

Trivandrum

Trivandrum or Thiruvananthapuram is the capital of Kerala State in the south of India. It is known for its calm beaches, backwaters, hill stations, and rich culture because of which it attracts several Indian as well as foreign tourists.
